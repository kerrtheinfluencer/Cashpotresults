Frequently Asked Questions
What played in Cash Pot today?
Cash Pot draws happen 6 times daily at 8:30am, 10:30am, 1:00pm, 3:00pm, 5:00pm and 8:25pm Jamaica time. CashPotJA updates within minutes of each draw.
How much does Cash Pot pay?
Cash Pot pays $260 for every $10 bet (26:1). With Mega Ball ($10 extra on top), you win $960 total. Monsta Ball adds even more on top.
What are the Cash Pot draw times?
6 draws daily:
• Early Bird — 8:30 AM
• Morning — 10:30 AM
• Midday — 1:00 PM
• Mid-Afternoon — 3:00 PM
• Drive Time — 5:00 PM
• Evening — 8:25 PM
What are the number meanings?
Each number 1-36 has a traditional Jamaican meaning (Afiyu Kent) used in dream interpretation. See the full chart in our Meanings tab.
How to get Cash Pot alerts?
Tap 🔔 Alerts in the header. You'll get an instant notification whenever new results drop.
