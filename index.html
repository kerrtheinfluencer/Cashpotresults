Frequently Asked Questions
What played in Cash Pot today?
Cash Pot draws happen 6 times daily in Jamaica at 8:30am, 10:30am, 1:00pm, 3:00pm, 5:00pm and 8:25pm (Jamaica time). Visit CashPotJA.com for the latest results — we update within minutes of each draw. Enable notifications to get alerted instantly when new numbers drop.
How much does Cash Pot pay?
Cash Pot pays $260 for every $10 bet (26:1 odds). With the Mega Ball add-on ($10 extra), you can win $960 total. The Monster Ball option is available after purchasing Mega Ball for even higher payouts.
What are the Cash Pot draw times?
There are 6 draws every day:
• Early Bird — 8:30 AM
• Morning — 10:30 AM
• Midday — 1:00 PM
• Mid-Afternoon — 3:00 PM
• Drive Time — 5:00 PM
• Evening — 8:25 PM
What do Cash Pot numbers mean?
Each number 1-36 has a traditional Jamaican meaning (Afiyu Kent). For example: 1=Duppy, 4=Egg, 11=Dog, 23=Black Man, 32=Gold. These meanings are used in dream interpretation — people play the number matching what they dreamed about. See the full chart in our Meanings tab.
