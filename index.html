Frequently Asked Questions
What played in Cash Pot today?
Cash Pot draws happen 6 times daily in Jamaica at 8:30am, 10:30am, 1:00pm, 3:00pm, 5:00pm and 8:25pm (Jamaica time). Visit CashPotJA.com for the latest results — we update within minutes of each draw.
How much does Cash Pot pay?
Cash Pot pays $260 for every $10 bet (26:1 odds). With the Mega Ball add-on ($10 extra), you can win $960 total.
What are the Cash Pot draw times?
There are 6 draws every day:
• Early Bird — 8:30 AM
• Morning — 10:30 AM
• Midday — 1:00 PM
• Mid-Afternoon — 3:00 PM
• Drive Time — 5:00 PM
• Evening — 8:25 PM
What do Cash Pot numbers mean?
Each number 1-36 has a traditional Jamaican meaning (Afiyu Kent). For example: 1=Duppy, 4=Egg, 11=Dog, 23=Black Man, 32=Gold. See the full chart in our Meanings tab.
How to get Cash Pot alerts?
Click the 🔔 Alerts button in the header to enable push notifications. You'll get an instant alert on your phone whenever new Cash Pot results are published.
